Common Questions

What Dealers Ask Before They Sign.

Straight answers — no marketing fog.

How fast can a PartSites store go live?

Standard build-to-launch is 30 days from OEM catalog hand-off — VIN decode, PAGETECH SEO surface, fraud shield, and checkout all live. Multi-store dealer groups typically go live in waves of 5–10 rooftops per month.

Does PartSites replace my current dealer website?

No — PartSites runs alongside it. Most dealers either (a) use PartSites as their parts subdomain (parts.dealersite.com) or (b) embed our EPIC widget into their existing Dealer.com / DealerInspire / custom site. No replatform required.

Which OEMs are supported?

Including but not limited to Volkswagen of America & Canada, Subaru of North America, Kia / Mobis, Nissan North America, and Chrysler / Mopar (PartSites powered the first Mopar DTC eStore). New OEM catalogs added on request.

How is PartSites priced?

Flat platform fee per store — no per-order transaction fees, no take-rate on parts sold, no per-SKU charges. Dealer groups get a network rate card. OEM programs get custom pricing.

Do you handle fulfillment?

No — fulfillment stays with the dealer (or the dealer group's central warehouse). PartSites runs the catalog, the storefront, the checkout, and the fraud shield. Your team ships the box.

Can I white-label PartSites for an OEM program?

Yes. PartSites powers both branded dealer eStores and white-labeled OEM programs — including a multi-year run with J.D. Power. Speak to us about a program build.
